Our Heritage

The Art of Coastal Living

Casa Solara stands as a testament to the timeless allure of Spanish architecture, reimagined for the vibrant spirit of Key West. Every archway, hand-painted tile, and sun-drenched terrace has been curated to offer a sense of profound tranquility.

Here, the boundary between indoors and out dissolves. Morning light filters through bougainvillea-draped balconies, while the scent of salt air mingles with the warmth of terracotta floors, creating an atmosphere that is both grand and intimately personal.

The Destination

Moments Beyond the Villa

While Casa Solara offers a world of its own, the magic of Key West awaits just beyond your gates. From the historic charm of Old Town to the crystal-clear waters of the Dry Tortugas, every moment is an invitation to explore.

  • Sunset sails on private yachts

    Experience the legendary Key West sunset from the deck of a luxury schooner.

  • Historic Old Town exploration

    Wander through streets lined with Victorian mansions and lush tropical gardens.

  • Local seafood fine dining

    Savor the catch of the day at world-class restaurants just minutes away.

  • Private deep-sea fishing charters

    Embark on an adventure to the world's third-largest barrier reef.
